Booking Info

Renting a car with us is easy and convenient! Here's how our simple booking process works:

Step 1: Choose Your Vehicle

Browse our fleet of vehicles and select the one that best suits your needs. Whether you're looking for a luxury car, an SUV, or a fuel-efficient sedan, we've got you covered.

Step 2: Select Rental Dates

Pick your preferred pickup and drop-off dates. Be sure to check availability for the dates you need to ensure we have the right car ready for you.

Step 3: Complete Your Booking

Finalize your booking by entering your details online, or feel free to contact us directly for assistance. We accept various payment options including credit cards and PayPal.

Step 4: Pick Up Your Car

Once your booking is confirmed, head to our location to pick up your vehicle. Our friendly staff will be there to assist you with any questions and make sure you're ready for your journey.

Payment Options & Cancellation Policy

We offer flexible payment methods for your convenience, including:

  • Credit Card
  • Debit Card
  • PayPal

Our cancellation policy allows for a full refund if canceled at least 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time. Cancellations made within 24 hours will incur a small fee.
